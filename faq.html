Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoBookr

How does AutoBookr work?

AutoBookr detects when you miss a call and instantly sends an automated SMS to the caller. The message can include a booking link, payment link, or custom response. You'll also receive a notification with the caller's details so you can follow up.

Do I need new phone hardware?

No! AutoBookr works with your existing phone line. Simply set up call forwarding or use our app to redirect calls. It's completely software-based - no extra equipment needed.

What happens if I answer the call?

If you answer the call, AutoBookr won't send an automated message. We only detect missed calls and calls that go to voicemail.

Can I customize the automated messages?

Yes! You can write your own custom messages or choose from our library of professionally written templates designed specifically for UK trade businesses.

Will I still get voicemail?

Yes, callers can still leave voicemail if they prefer. AutoBookr adds an extra layer of lead capture by responding to missed calls with a text.

Can I use my existing number?

Yes, you can keep your current business number. We simply add call detection to your existing line through call forwarding or our mobile app.

How quickly are messages sent?

Messages are sent within seconds of a missed call being detected. Most customers receive their text within 30 seconds.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required to start. You can test the full service before committing.

What are the costs beyond the monthly fee?

The monthly fee covers everything - no hidden costs. Standard SMS rates apply, but these are included in your plan. There are no setup fees or additional charges.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. Simply contact our support team or use your account dashboard.

Do you offer support?

We offer email support on all plans, with priority support on Growth plans and dedicated account management on Pro plans. Our team is UK-based and responsive.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take data security seriously and comply with UK data protection regulations. Your customer data is encrypted and never shared with third parties.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help. Get in touch and we'll answer your questions.
