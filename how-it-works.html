How AutoBookr Works

Three simple steps to never miss another customer call

The Process

1

Call Detection

When a customer calls and you don't answer (or lines are busy), our system instantly detects the missed call through your existing phone line.

2

Automated SMS

Within seconds, we send a personalized text message to the caller thanking them for their interest and providing immediate next steps.

3

Instant Notification

You receive a notification with the caller's details, so you can follow up quickly and convert the lead into a booked job.

What Happens Next?

Customer Responds

If the customer replies to the SMS, you get instant notification with their message and can respond directly.

Click to Book

Include a booking link in your automated message so customers can schedule appointments with one tap.

Payment Option

With Growth and Pro plans, include Stripe payment links for deposits or immediate payments.

Follow-Up Reminder

Set automatic follow-up reminders if customers don't reply, ensuring no lead goes cold.
