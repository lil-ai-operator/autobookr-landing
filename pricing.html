Why AutoBookr?

Missed calls are lost revenue

  • Most customers call 2–3 businesses, and book the first one that replies.
  • If you miss the call while on-site, that lead often goes cold in minutes.
  • AutoBookr sends an instant text so you stay in the race for the job.

Manual follow-up kills your day

  • No more end-of-day call backs to people who already hired someone else.
  • Automatic replies handle first contact while you stay focused on paid work.
  • Your team gets cleaner, more qualified leads to follow up.

Built for UK trades, not generic SMBs

  • Fast setup, simple plans, and no long lock-in contracts.

FAQs

Will this actually get me more jobs?

Yes — speed wins. When a caller gets an instant reply, you're far more likely to keep the lead and book the work before they move on.

What if I'm not technical?

No problem. Setup is straightforward, and most trade businesses are live in under 24 hours with guided onboarding.

What if I already have a receptionist or office admin?

AutoBookr supports them — it doesn't replace them. It catches missed calls instantly so your team can focus on high-value follow-up.

Is there a contract or long-term lock-in?

No long contracts. Monthly plans, cancel anytime, and keep full control as your business grows.
