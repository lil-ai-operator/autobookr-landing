Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. We'll pro-rate your final month.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans so you can test the service before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers through our secure Stripe payment system.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Pay annually and get 2 months free. That's 16 months for the price of 12.
