About AutoBookr

Helping UK trade businesses never miss a customer call

Our Story

AutoBookr was founded in 2024 by a team of ex-tradespeople who understood the frustration of missing calls while on the job. We saw too many small businesses lose potential customers simply because they couldn't answer the phone at that moment.

We built AutoBookr to solve this problem - a simple, affordable tool that ensures every potential customer gets an instant response, every time.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: help UK trade businesses never miss a customer call again. We believe that every enquiry deserves a response, and every missed call is a missed opportunity.

We're passionate about supporting the trades that keep Britain running - from plumbers and electricians to builders and decorators.
